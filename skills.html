Effective Communication Skills

Learn and develop key communication skills for success in both personal and professional settings.

Focus fully on the speaker, avoid interrupting, and show interest in what they are saying. Reflect back what you've heard to confirm understanding.

Be clear and concise in your messages. Use simple language to avoid misunderstandings, and get to the point without unnecessary details.

Try to understand the feelings and perspectives of others. Show genuine concern for their needs and viewpoints to build trust and rapport.

Be mindful of body language, facial expressions, and tone of voice. Non-verbal cues can convey as much information as spoken words.

Give feedback that is positive, specific, and focused on behaviors rather than personal attributes. Aim to help others improve constructively.
